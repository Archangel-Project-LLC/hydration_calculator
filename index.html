Post-Exercise Rehydration
Aim to consume ~150% of fluid lost during exercise within 4-6 hours post-training
Lost 1kg during training? Drink 1.5L in the 4-6 hours after.
Why 150%? You continue losing fluid through sweat and urine after training. 150% accounts for ongoing losses and ensures full rehydration ‚Äî especially important if you're training again within 12 hours.
